Business Model.

Time & Material

This model is similar to a service or a manpower consulting model where after understanding client requirements we designate one of our writers to be placed at the client location. The experience and skill level of the writer is arrived upon after understanding the client requirements.

The resource will be billed on a monthly basis. The monthly cost will also include the services of a senior manager and editor who will oversee the work on a regular basis. The duration of this business model depends on the project requirement. The minimum period of engagement is three months.


Fixed Bid

In this model our business analysts will visit the client, gather requirements, estimate the time and effort required and arrive at a fixed cost for the project. The project can be executed at the client premises or at our office. In this model we clearly communicate to the client the following points:


- Scope of the work

- The limitations of the deliverables

- Preview of the deliverables

- Timeframe

- Levels of review (Technical/Grammatical)

- Software tools to be used

Expect the expected when you meet people with the relavant experience.
